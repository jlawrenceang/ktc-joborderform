- Contact the DPO for any privacy concern or to exercise your rights (Section 8).

## 2. Personal Data We Collect

When you register and use the Portal, we collect:

- **Identity and contact data** — full name, email address, and account credentials.
- **A valid government-issued ID** that you upload (image or PDF). A government ID is **regulated personal data** and is treated with heightened care.
- **Broker/company details** — e.g. broker code, company name, where provided.
- **Job order data** — consignee, entry numbers, container numbers, and requested services you submit.
- **Technical data** — limited security and usage data needed to operate the Portal (e.g. authentication and anti-abuse/CAPTCHA signals).

## 3. Purposes of Processing

We process your personal data to:

- Register, verify, and manage your broker account, including **verifying your identity** from the valid ID you upload.
- Review and decide on your accreditation/approval.
- Process and manage your Job Orders and provide terminal services.
- Communicate with you about your account, applications, and orders.
- Maintain security, prevent fraud and abuse, and keep records.
- Comply with legal, regulatory, and lawful government requirements (e.g. Bureau of Customs, PDEA).

## 4. Legal Basis

We process your data based on:

- **Your consent** (which you give at registration), for identity verification using your uploaded ID and related processing.
- **Performance of a contract / steps at your request** — to provide the Portal and terminal services.
- **Compliance with legal obligations** and KTC's legitimate interests in operating and securing the terminal, balanced against your rights.

## 5. Storage, Security, and Service Providers

- Your data is stored in our managed cloud backend (**Supabase** — database and file storage) and the Portal is hosted on **Vercel**, with anti-abuse provided by **Cloudflare Turnstile**. These providers act as our processors.
- Uploaded IDs are kept in restricted storage; access is limited to **authorized KTC personnel**, retrieved via expiring secure links. Data is encrypted in transit.
- We apply reasonable organizational, physical, and technical security measures as required by the DPA.

## 6. Disclosure and Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may disclose it:

- To our **service providers/processors** (above) strictly to operate the Portal.
- To **government authorities or regulators** (e.g. BOC, PDEA, NPC, law enforcement) when required by law or lawful order.
- Where necessary to **establish, exercise, or defend legal claims**, or protect rights and safety.

## 7. International Transfers

- Our cloud providers may process or store data on servers **located outside the Philippines**. Where this occurs, KTC takes steps consistent with the DPA to ensure your data remains protected.

## 8. Retention

- We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active and as needed for the purposes above, and thereafter only as required by law or for legitimate business and legal purposes. Specific retention periods: **[TO BE SET BY KTC]**.
- When no longer needed, data is securely deleted or anonymized.

## 9. Your Rights as a Data Subject

Under the Data Privacy Act, you have the right to:

- **Be informed** about the processing of your personal data.
- **Access** your personal data and obtain a copy.
- **Rectify** inaccurate or outdated data.
- **Object** to or **withdraw consent** for processing (which may limit or end your use of the Portal).
- **Erasure or blocking** of your data under the conditions provided by law.
- **Data portability** for data processed by electronic means.
- **File a complaint** with the National Privacy Commission, and to be **indemnified** for damages under the law.

To exercise these rights, contact our DPO (Section 1). We may need to verify your identity before acting on a request.

## 10. Withdrawal of Consent

- You may withdraw your consent at any time by contacting the DPO. Withdrawal does not affect processing done before withdrawal, and may mean we can no longer verify your identity or provide Portal access.

## 11. Changes to this Notice

- We may update this Notice. Material changes will be posted on the Portal with an updated version number, and we may ask you to re-confirm your consent.

## 12. Consent

By ticking the privacy-consent box at registration, you confirm that you have read and understood this Notice and **consent to KTC's collection and processing of your personal data, including the valid government-issued ID you upload**, for the purposes described above.
